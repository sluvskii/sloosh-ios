# NeoMovies Android v0.1.0-pre1

The first prerelease of the NeoMovies mobile client. At this stage the project got its initial Android build, its core app structure, and the first public packages for testing.

## What was included

- an initial mobile client for content browsing;
- the first app navigation flow;
- early public builds for manual testing.

## Why it mattered

With `v0.1.0-pre1`, the project moved beyond an internal prototype and got its first public Android build. That became the base for the later TV branch, TorrServer support, and interface improvements.
