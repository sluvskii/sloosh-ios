# NeoMovies is now in Telegram

NeoMovies now has its own Telegram bot for watching movies, series, and anime through embedded players and directly inside Telegram.

## What is available

- quick access to the catalog without a separate app;
- playback through Telegram or external delivery options;
- a dedicated collection that will continue to grow.
