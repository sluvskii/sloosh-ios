# NeoMovies Android v0.1.0-pre3

The third prerelease focused on the TV side of the app. The main goal was to make the TV player usable day to day and fix the issues that were blocking stream startup.

## What changed

- added a TV UI wrapper around the player;
- fixed TV player bugs;
- improved the TV watch selector and TV app flow;
- fixed TorrServer issues and several smaller UI bugs.

## Result

`v0.1.0-pre3` stabilized the TV direction and prepared the project for a larger batch of playback and details-screen fixes in the next prerelease.
